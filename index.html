Our Goal

The Fourth Trimester Project is a groundbreaking initiative based in the State of Ohio, serving the cities of Akron and Canton. Our project aims to improve maternal and infant health outcomes by addressing the critical yet often overlooked postpartum period. We envision the establishment of a dedicated postpartum home that provides mothers with a nurturing and supportive environment to receive comprehensive respite care. This one-pager highlights the importance of postpartum support and explains why the creation of such a facility is essential. The Fourth Trimester Project will offer a wide range of services, including overnight respite care, nutritious meals, emotional support, and classes on vital topics such as lactation support, newborn milestones, parenting, yoga, meditation, sound therapy, and self-care. Furthermore, as the project evolves, it will expand to include childcare, spa, and beauty services.

The Importance of
Postpartum Support

The postpartum period, commonly known as the fourth trimester, is a critical stage in a mother's journey towards recovery and adjustment after childbirth . It is a time of significant physical, emotional, and psychological changes, and adequate support during this period is crucial for the well-being of both mothers and infants. Our project aims to focus on four areas: Enhancing maternal health, fostering infant health, providing emotional well-being support, and offering education that empowers mothers, and their families
